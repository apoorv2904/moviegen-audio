What Can the Movie Gen Audio Model Do?

The Movie Gen Audio model offers the following capabilities:

  • Better Synchronization: Achieves far better synchronization than any existing video-to-SFX models.
  • Music: Composes music based on videos, taking sentiments into account to enhance emotional impact.
  • SFX and Music: Generates sound effects (SFX) and music jointly, blending them harmoniously like a professional studio.
  • Non-Diegetic Sound Effects: Injects non-diegetic sound effects, such as "whoosh" sounds when changing scenes, to elevate the cinematic experience.
  • Audio Infilling: Allows targeted modifications given the audio-video context to support edits without disrupting the overall soundtrack.
  • Longform Soundtrack: Creates soundtracks that are longer than two minutes, suitable for short films and extended video content.
