Movie Gen Audio: Bringing Life to the Videos

This blog presents a summary of the audio part of the Movie Gen paper paper. Movie Gen Audio is a text conditioned video-to-audio model designed to generate soundtracks for both video clips and short films ranging from a few seconds to several minutes. Our primary objectives include creating ambient sounds, sound effects (Foley), and instrumental music that align with each scene, and blend professionally to provide a cinematic experience. By training on large amounts of data and model scaling, Movie Gen Audio model that learns to design sounds like a human, elevating the overall impact of videos.
